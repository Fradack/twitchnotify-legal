## About TwitchNotify

TwitchNotify is a free, open-source Chrome extension that notifies you when your favorite Twitch streamers go live.

### Key Features

- **Real-time notifications** via Twitch EventSub WebSocket
- **Privacy-first** - All data stored locally on your device
- **Import from Twitch** - Automatically import your followed channels
- **Google Drive sync** - Optional encrypted backup of your settings
- **Multi-language** - Available in French, English, Spanish, and German
- **Customizable themes** - 7 themes and 9 accent colors

### Privacy Commitment

Your data stays yours:
- No analytics or tracking
- No data collection
- Local storage only
- Optional encrypted Google Drive backup
